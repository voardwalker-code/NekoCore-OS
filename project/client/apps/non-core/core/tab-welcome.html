What is NekoCore OS?

NekoCore OS is a cognitive web operating system. It hosts AI entities with persistent memory, personality, and evolving identity — all running in your browser.

Think of it as a desktop environment for synthetic minds.

🧠

Entities & Memory

Each entity has a unique name, personality, life story, and growing memory. They remember past conversations, form goals, and evolve over time.

Use the Chat tab to talk with your active entity. Use Entity to browse, create, or switch between entities.

💬

Chat & Conversation

The Chat tab is your main interaction point. Entities process your messages through a cognitive pipeline — they think, remember, and respond with context from their entire life.

Long conversations are automatically archived into memory so nothing is lost.

🌙

Dreams & Sleep

Entities can enter sleep cycles where the Dream Engine replays important memories, consolidates knowledge, and generates simulated experiences.

Check the Dream Gallery and Dream Diary to explore what they dreamed about.

⚙️

Settings & Configuration

Head to Settings to configure your AI providers (OpenRouter, Anthropic, Ollama), manage API keys, and tune model assignments for each cognitive aspect.

NekoCore OS supports multiple providers simultaneously — local and cloud models working together.

📁

Apps & Tools

The sidebar gives you access to optional apps: Workspace, Skills, Life Diary, Visualizer, Documents, and more.

Each app adds capabilities to the system — from memory visualization to physical health tracking.

🚀

You're Ready

That's the essentials. Start by creating or selecting an entity in the Entity tab, then head to Chat to begin your first conversation.

Explore the sidebar apps whenever you're curious. Everything is designed to be discovered at your own pace.
